Privacy Policy

This document explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using TrojanHorse software, website, and Discord bot.

Information We Collect

TrojanHorse does not intentionally collect personal information from visitors of this website.

Discord Bot Data

The TrojanHorse Discord bot may collect limited data required for operation:

  • Discord user IDs
  • Server (guild) IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Message content only when required for commands or moderation

Use of Data

Collected data is used strictly to operate and improve the Service, ensure functionality, and prevent abuse.

Data Sharing

TrojanHorse does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties. Data may only be disclosed if required by law or to comply with Discord’s Terms of Service.

Data Retention

Data is stored only for as long as necessary to provide the Service and is deleted when no longer required.

Children’s Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. No data is knowingly collected from children.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact
